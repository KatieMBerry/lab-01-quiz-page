A little bit about Argentina...

Argentina is a vast land that covers most of the southern half of South America. As the eighth largest country in the world, and spanning an area bigger than Texas and Mexico combined, it is home to rich and diverse landscapes and ecosystems.

Landscapes

Some of the landscapes Argentina is best known-for include:

  • Andes: The highest elevation in the Andes is Mount Aconcagua, which is 22,841 feet above sea level!

  • Bueno Aires: "The Paris of South America"

  • Patagonia: Areas consisting of desolate desert, Andean forests, and dense marine life

Biodiversity

In Argentina, native animals include:

  1. Penguins
  2. Jaguars
  3. Guanacos
